“The Gospel in the Book of Revelation”

This series of videos is a guided tour through the Book of Revelation in 18 sessions, each about 20 minutes long. They are provided by Trinity School for Ministry in Ambridge, Pennsylvania, where they were recorded in 2007. Be sure to download the Session Outlines pdf through the link under the link on the website containing the series of videos.
