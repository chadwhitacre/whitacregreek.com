The Jesus Prayer

The Jesus Prayer is a form of prayer long valued in the Eastern Church which has become increasingly known in the other churches. “Hallowing the Name: The Jesus Prayer as a Sacramental Practice for Prayer and Daily Life” explores the Jesus Prayer in the light of Scripture and in relation to other forms of prayer, as well as a life of discipleship in general.
